  • IWDT_DIVIDER_8 : prescaler divider equal to 8
  • IWDT_DIVIDER_16 : prescaler divider equal to 16
  • IWDT_DIVIDER_32 : prescaler divider equal to 32
  • IWDT_DIVIDER_64 : prescaler divider equal to 64
  • IWDT_DIVIDER_128: prescaler divider equal to 128
  • IWDT_DIVIDER_256: prescaler divider equal to 256
